🔎 Information & privacy leverage

Know what a new employer can actually verify — employment dates and past salary (often via Equifax's The Work Number) — and where laws let you decline to share salary history. You can even freeze your Work Number data.

🎛️ Levers beyond base pay

Base is one number of many. Equity, sign-on, title/level, remote, start date, and review timing are all negotiable — sometimes when base isn't.

🏢 Internal moves

Leverage without leaving: counteroffers, internal transfers, promotion-cycle timing, and skip-level conversations.

Know your rights on background/credit checks (FCRA), handle references well, and get every offer in writing.
